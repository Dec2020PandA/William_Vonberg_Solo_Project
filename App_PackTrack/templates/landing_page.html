What is Pack Track?

Pack Track is an easy and effective way to track not only your basic macros and activities, but to track your best friends as well! Now you can stay on top of your fitness goals while helping your buddy stay in shape too.

How does it work?

It's easy! All you have to do is record your macros after you consume them and enter in your activity and calories burned after you do them, we calculate the rest. We then provide you a summary based on your goals.

How much does it cost?

Nothing! This is a free to use app that you can utilize to help you in your fitness and macro tracking. No subscription needed at this time! How's that for easy!
